Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AutoGlob ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit autoglob.co or use any related services.

Information we collect

  • Information you provide. If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect the email address you give us.

  • Cookies and similar technologies. We use cookies for site functionality, analytics, and advertising. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

How we use information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the site
  • To send newsletter content to subscribers who opt in

Third-party advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings, or by visiting aboutads.info if you are outside the EEA.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers who help us operate the site — such as hosting, analytics, and email delivery providers — solely for the purpose of providing those services.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and to opt out of certain data processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

Children's privacy

AutoGlob is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
